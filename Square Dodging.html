    Game Rules & Leaderboard

    Player: You are the white square. Move your mouse to control it. The dot will follow your mouse.

    Scoring: Your score goes up by 1 every second. Survive for as long as possible!

    Enemies:

    • Red Blocks: Deal 1 to 5 damage, depending on their size.
    • Yellow Blocks: Appear in Round 2 and beyond. Deal 5 to 10 damage, depending on their size.

    Rounds: Each round lasts for one minute. As rounds progress, more blocks will fly at you.

    Health: You start with 100 health. Every 5 rounds, you gain a health bonus, starting at +50 and decreasing by 1 each time.

    Future Updates

    • **Superpowers:** Collect special power-ups like temporary invincibility, a freeze-time ability, or a score multiplier.
    • **New Enemies:** Introduce new enemy types, like fast-moving triangles or exploding shapes.
    • **Customization:** Unlock different colors and shapes for your player by reaching certain scores.
    • **Multiplayer Mode:** Compete against other players in real-time on a single, shared map.
